Pokémon Game Instructions

How to Play

  1. Enter the map with Pokeballs scattered randomly.
  2. Move to a Pokeball to catch a Pokémon.
  3. Choose your Pokémon:
    • Continue to Battle: Use your caught Pokémon to battle a random Pokémon.
    • Try Again: Catch another Pokémon if you're not satisfied.
  4. Choose moves to attack your opponent during a battle.
  5. Catch more Pokémon and battle through the game.
  6. If your Pokémon's HP reaches zero, you'll be transported back to the map.

Tips

  • Pay attention to Pokémon stats.
  • Experiment with moves during battles.

Enjoy the adventure!
